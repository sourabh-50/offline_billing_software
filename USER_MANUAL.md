# Premium Billing System - User Manual

Welcome to your professional billing system. This software is designed for simplicity, speed, and reliability.

## 1. Getting Started
- **Login**: If a password is set, enter it to access the dashboard.
- **Home Screen**: View your total sales today, this week, and this month at a glance.

## 2. Creating an Invoice (New Bill)
1.  Go to the **New Bill** tab.
2.  **Customer Details**: Enter the customer's name, mobile number, and address.
3.  **Mobile Details**:
    - Click **+ Add Mobile** to add multiple items.
    - Enter the Model, IMEI, Battery, and Charger numbers.
    - Enter the price. The total will update automatically.
4.  **Save & Print**: Click the "Save & Print" button. The PDF will be generated and opened automatically for printing.

## 3. Searching & Reports
- **Search Bills**: Find any old bill by searching for the Customer Name, Invoice Number, or Mobile Number.
- **Reports**: Generate PDF sales reports for any date range (Today, Last Week, etc.).

## 4. Data Backup (Protect your data!)
- Go to the **Backup** tab.
- **Excel Backup**: Export your sales data to an Excel file for accounting.
- **PDF Archive**: Combine all your bills for a month into a single PDF file for easy storage.
- **Full Backup**: Creates a ZIP file of your entire database and all invoices. Keep this on a USB drive!

## 5. Personalization & Settings
- **Security**: Update or remove your login password.
- **Maintenance**: Clear old data if the system feels slow (Caution: This is permanent).
- **Appearance**: Switch between Light Mode and Dark Mode.
